About Me

Hello! I'm José Elias !

Passionate about development since I was 18, Brazilian and born in 1980. I have a degree in Internet Systems from Faculdade Católica Imaculada Conceição do Recife (FICR), I have certification from Qualiti Innovative Learning, Certifications from the Instituto Federal do Rio Grande do Sul. I am a person who loves challenges, just like every developer.

I like people who take me out of my comfort zone, because my greatest developments and learnings come from moments when I was forced to leave it and face life. I am an avid student who enjoys drinking coffee in my free time. I watch action movies and series, and I also like going to the movies and watching soccer matches.
